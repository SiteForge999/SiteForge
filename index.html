Help & Instructions

Learn how to build, deploy, and configure your website

1. How to add and edit content?

Click any block in the 'Blocks' tab to add it to the canvas. Click any text directly on the screen to change it, or click images directly to change their URLs.

2. How to configure links on buttons?

Select a block on the canvas, go to the 'Styles' tab, and scroll to 'Block Content Customization'. Enter the target URL in the 'Button Link URL' field. You can write external URLs or anchor tags like '#pricing'.

3. How to make the contact form work?

Select the contact form block. In the Styles tab under content customization, paste a form submission endpoint (e.g. from Formspree.io). The form on your exported website will submit messages directly to your email.

4. How to export and host for free?

Click the 'Export HTML' button in the top right. It will compile your layout and download it as an 'index.html' file. You can drag and drop this file into Netlify or upload it to GitHub Pages for 100% free hosting.
